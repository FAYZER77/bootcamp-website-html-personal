My Top 3 Movies of all-time

The foudational movie of the best fantasy trilogy ever made. The heart displayed in the extended version in particular is what makes the rest of the films worth sticking around for

Slow paced character study that is as realistic as it is disturbing in it's examination of a character who is the personification of capitalism

A brave film for the era it was created due to isn't psuedo romantic undertones between men along with a brooding sensibility in reflecting on immortality.
